"The Fire Within"

Ananya Verma

Published: March 2023 | 4 min read

Feminist Poetry | Social Justice

"A fiery manifesto challenging patriarchal norms through verse"

My voice, once ember in the night,
Now flames that pierce through society's blight,
In every verse, a battle won,
The revolution has begun.

The crackle of pages turning into weapons,
Words sharpened on the whetstone of oppression,
Each stanza a marching beat,
Our pens drumming revolution's heat.
