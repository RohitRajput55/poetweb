Our Story of Empowerment

Since 2018, Delhi Kavitri Manch has been revolutionizing women's voices through poetry, creating ripples of change across India's literary landscape.

Our Journey

2018 - The Beginning

Founded with just 12 women poets in Delhi

2019 - First Anthology

Published our first collection of feminist poetry

2020 - National Recognition

Featured in India Today's "Social Change Makers" list

2022 - Rural Expansion

Launched poetry workshops in 5 rural communities

2023 - 5000+ Members
